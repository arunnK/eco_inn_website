1. Introduction

       With the ground water table exhausting in the rural india due to excessive pressure on ground water table by agricultural irrigation, there is a growing need to closely and effectively monitor the irrigation process . Every year nearly 2 lakh litres of ground water is wasted due to over or improper irrigation of agricultural lands . To overcome this problem our team has developed a handy and extremely cheap wireless module for the better monitoring and convenience of the large farmers.Apart from this we also aim to develop a system through which farmers can irrigate their fields 24 *7 and optionaly run their motors on single phase through same wireless module during non-availability of 3 phase supply.

2. Product

Our aim is to directly interface the the 3 phase induction motor starter with a mobile phone of the user using GSM module and DTMF board centrally controlled by a ATMEGA 16 microcontroller as follows:

  1. A call is placed to GSM module using a mobile phone (available with the farmer already).
  2. Now this GSM module is connected to a DTMF module as shown

    3. This module converts the frequency of sound corresponding to each no. and gives instructions input to ATMEGA 16 microntroller which controls the 3/1 phase supply to the motor to perform various operations as under:

    1. turn on motor.
    2. turn on motor for specific period.
    3. turn off the motor.
    4. run motor on single phase.
    5. un the motor on 3 phase.

3. Specifications

Components used; ATMEGA 16 compatible GSM module,DTMF board,ATMEGA 16 microcontroller.

4. Advantages:

  1. Affordable @ Rs 1000 on mass production.
  2. Range limited by the signal strength of mobile service company. nearly infinite range of operation.
  3. Irrigation is possible 24x7.
  4. Robust and can work in harsh condition.
  5. Can also be superseded manually under emergency conditions or failure.
