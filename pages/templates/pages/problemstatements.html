Current Problem Statements

Educational App Development

A dedicated team of CSE students are designing a mobile-based platform that categorizes the course material for different academic subjects according to different academic levels. The aim for developing such an application is to have a common platform where teachers, aspiring teachers, students, and those stung by the curiosity bug can download education material, and share their own views, experiences, and feedbacks online in local languages.

Internet based security system for Crop Protection

This is a joint project of ECE and CSE students who aim at providing a solution for crop protection for farmers.Animal attack on crops is one of the major problem faced by farmers. These attacks mainly by monkeys and cows cause a lot of loss to the farmers. The existing solution are mainly Electric fence, ultrasonic buzzers or there is a person present in the farms. These are not much effective. We propose an electronic system to safeguard the farms from animals. It will remove the need of fencing and persons to be present at the farms.
